References

The algorithm used in Wisent to generate the parser is based on the
following article:

    * David Pager, A practical general method for constructing LR(k) parsers.
      Acta Informatica, volume 7 (1977), number 3, pages 249

More references are:

    * Wikipedia has pages about `context free grammars`_, `LR parsers`_
      and Wisents.

    .. _context free grammars: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Context-free_grammar
    .. _LR parsers: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LR_parser
    .. Wisents: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wisent

    * The Bison_ parser generator.

    .. _Bison: http://www.gnu.org/software/bison/
