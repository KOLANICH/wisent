**********
References
**********

The algorithm used in Wisent to generate the parser is based on the
following article:

  * David Pager, *A practical general method for constructing LR(k)
    parsers*.  Acta Informatica, volume 7 (1977), number 3, pages 249-268,
    DOI `10.1007/BF00290336`_.

    .. _10.1007/BF00290336: http://dx.doi.org/10.1007/BF00290336

More references are:

  * Wikipedia has articles about `context free grammars`_, `LR
    parsers`_ and Wisents_.

    .. _context free grammars: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Context-free_grammar
    .. _LR parsers: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LR_parser
    .. _Wisents: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wisent

  * The first edition of the book *Parsing Techniques --- A
    Practical Guide* by Dick Grune and Ceriel J.H. Jacobs is
    `available online`_.

    .. _available online: http://www.cs.vu.nl/~dick/PTAPG.html

  * The Bison_ parser generator is a good parser generator for C and
    C++ projects.  Bison comes with an excellent manual.

    .. _Bison: http://www.gnu.org/software/bison/

  * The LanguageParsing_ entry on wiki.python.org_ lists other
    Python parser generators.

    .. _LanguageParsing: http://wiki.python.org/moin/LanguageParsing
    .. _wiki.python.org: http://wiki.python.org/

  * Xkcd `knows regular expressions`_.

    .. _knows regular expressions: http://xkcd.com/208/
